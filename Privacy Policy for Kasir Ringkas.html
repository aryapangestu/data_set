Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 3, 2024

This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use Kasir Ringkas ("the Application"). It explains your privacy rights and the protections in place for your data.

Note: Kasir Ringkas is an offline application that primarily stores all data locally on your device. An internet connection is only required during the initial activation process for licensing purposes.

Interpretation and Definitions

Interpretation
Words with capitalized letters have defined meanings as explained below, and these definitions apply whether they appear in singular or plural form.

Definitions

  • Account: A unique account created to access specific features of the Application.
  • Application: Refers to Kasir Ringkas, provided by the Company.
  • Company: The entity that owns and manages Kasir Ringkas (referred to as "We," "Us," or "Our").
  • Country: Indonesia.
  • Device: Any electronic device capable of running the Application, such as a smartphone or tablet.
  • Personal Data: Any information relating to an identifiable individual.
  • Service: Refers to the features provided by the Application.
  • You: The user or individual using the Application.

Collecting and Using Your Data

Types of Data Collected

  • Local Storage Data: Kasir Ringkas uses local storage on your device to manage your data securely without sending it over the internet. This includes transaction records, inventory, and user settings.
  • Activation Data: During initial setup, we require an internet connection to activate your license. This may involve collecting and sending basic information, such as device identifiers and license details, to our secure servers.
  • Device Permissions: Kasir Ringkas may request permission to access specific device features, such as the camera, to support scanning barcodes. This data is not sent to external servers and remains on your device.

Use of Your Data

We use data collected solely for the purpose of:

  • Activating your license and verifying your usage rights.
  • Maintaining and improving the functionality of the Application.
  • Providing support and troubleshooting as requested.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data generated within Kasir Ringkas is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit, store, or back up this data on external servers.
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your local data. However, the security of data on your device also depends on the measures you take, such as using device-level encryption or secure passwords.

Children's Privacy

Our Application is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you become aware that a child under 13 has used the Application and provided personal data, please contact us to have the data removed.

Internet Usage

Kasir Ringkas operates offline, except for the first-time license activation. Once the license is activated, all core functionalities work without an internet connection. Ensure your device is secure and that you understand the implications of using external networks when activating the license.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will update the "Last updated" date accordingly. We recommend reviewing the Privacy Policy regularly for any updates.
